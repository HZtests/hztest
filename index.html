HZ's Contractor LLC 

HZ's Contractor LLC provides top quality work at reasonable prices. We provide all levels of service from complete buildings to minor repairs. HZ's Contractor specializes in custom framing, Add-a-Level, basement refinishing, Out Buildings, new Garages, Pergolas, Cabanas, custom decks, custom aluminum work, Roofing, Gutters, siding installation and repairs. If you have a new project or repair we do it all so complete the contact form and we will get back to you and complete your project quickly with top notch quality. We are a NJ licensed contractor and fully insured.
