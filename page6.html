Need Porch Work?

HZ's Contractor can provide you a new or renovated front, back or side porch. We can do all the work to remove and build a new porch or renovate your existing porch as well. We can provide everything including framing, painting, lighting, masonry work and trim and custom aluminum. Look below to see the general steps in a new porch or renovation. We provide quality work at reasonable rates so your dream porch is within your budget. Complete the contact form and we will get back to start planning your project. We are a NJ licensed contractor and fully insured.

Porch Work Photo Gallery

HZ's Contractor LLC can provide you with a complete new porch, a remodeled porch and even a custom design. Generally a porch project happens per the following general steps:

  1. Planning and design (we can help get all of this in place)
  2. Demo and removal of all existing things that will no longer be used
  3. Major masonry items such as footings, block work or other foundation items
  4. Framing which includes any tie-in to the house or roof
  5. Siding repairs, flooring, ceiling components and others as required 
  6. Finishing such as painting, aluminum work, trim, doors and stairs (if required)
  7. Complete utilities such as electrical (as required)

We can create a new space or renovate your current porch to make it the envy of your neighborhood. The steps above are used when renovating or building most porches. Contact Us to have us help you make your old space the new space you have always wanted at a price that will make it a realty.
