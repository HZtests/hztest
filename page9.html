Need Repairs Done?

HZ's Contractor can provide you with all of the repairs and maintenance that you need for your home or business. We do all types of work from gutter, roof or siding repairs to renovate, rebuild or do custom construction. We provide everything from framing, painting, masonry, trim, doors and custom aluminum. Look below to see a list and pictures of some of the repairs we do. We provide quality work at reasonable rates in a fast timeline. Repair and Maintenance Work Photo Gallery

HZ's Contractor LLC can provide you with a complete maintenance and repair service for your home or business. We provide maintenance services such as gutter cleaning, clean outs, deck cleaning and inspections and also repairs or replacement of roofs, gutters, windows, doors siding, masonry, decks, baths, kitchens, sheetrock, trim, painting, flooring, porches, basements and much more. If you have something in mind contact us and we will let you know what we can do and when we will have it done. Generally repairs and maintenance have the following general steps:

  1. Planning and schedule (we can help get things done quickly)
  2. Free estimate and order of parts with deposit (if required) if no parts required we can usually start right away
  3. We will come at the agreed upon time and complete the work
  4. We will finish, clean up and be ready for the next time you have a repair and call

We can help you maintain and repair many things at your home or business. The steps above are for most general repairs. Contact Us to have us help you make your "Honey Do List" disappear.
