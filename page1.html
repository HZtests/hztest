New Construction Photo Gallery

HZ's Contractor LLC can provide you with a complete new building or any part of your new building project. For a complete new building the general steps are:

  1. General Excavation, footings and foundation walls
  2. Framing which includes sills, walls, beams, joists and roof assemblies
  3. Sheathing on the walls and roof
  4. Roofing, windows, doors, siding and gutters (if required)
  5. Interior finishing such as sheetrock, trim, interior doors and stairs (if required)
  6. Complete utilities such as electrical, plumbing and HVAC (if required)

We can do some or the entire project and the steps above are used when building most new buildings such as Garages, Out Buildings, Pool Houses, Barns or Large Sheds. Contact Us to have us help you make you new building dreams a reality.
